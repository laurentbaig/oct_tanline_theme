Welcome to Tanline Printing!

We are a Tucson-based print shop and we love printing. Our shop includes screenprinting, letterpress, risograph, direct-to-garment printing, sublimation printing, and embroidery.

Headed by co-owners Jeik Ficker and Amanda Beekhuizen, we are a community of artists working together. We believe in the power of printmaking, self-publishing, and collaboration, and we sell special goods that we make with friends. 

We also provide custom printing services; classes in screen printing, letterpress, and bookmaking; live printing at events; collaboration with artists to publish their works as prints and books; and monthly exhibitions at our gallery, Tiny Town Gallery.
