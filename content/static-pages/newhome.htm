Welcome to Tanline Printing!

We are a Tucson-based print shop and we love printing. Our shop includes screenprinting, letterpress, risograph, direct-to-garment printing, sublimation printing, and embroidery. We print shirts, hats, stickers, garments, prints on paper, and much more.

Headed by co-owners Jeik Ficker and Amanda Beekhuizen, we run Tanline Printing where we do all kinds of custom work, and we also print goodies for our retail store, Tiny Town Surplus, located on Historic Fourth Avenue in Tucson.
